Faculty Contributions to CDC

The faculty members of SVNIT play a pivotal role in the Career Development Cell (CDC), contributing their expertise and efforts to enhance student career opportunities, industry collaborations, and skill development programs. Their work encompasses organizing training sessions, facilitating internships, engaging with recruiters, and mentoring students to prepare them for successful professional careers.

Faculty Highlight: Prof. (Dr.) H. B. Mehta

Prof. (Dr.) H. B. Mehta
Professor Incharge
Prof. Mehta leads the Career Development Cell (CDC) at SVNIT, spearheading initiatives to enhance student career prospects. His achievements include:
  • Establishing strong industry partnerships to facilitate internships and placements.
  • Organizing skill development workshops and training programs tailored to industry needs.
  • Mentoring students and guiding them through career planning and professional development.
  • Successfully increasing campus placement rates through strategic recruiter engagement.
